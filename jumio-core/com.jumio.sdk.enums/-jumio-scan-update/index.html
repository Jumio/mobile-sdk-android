JumioScanUpdate

enum JumioScanUpdate : Enum<JumioScanUpdate>

JumioScanUpdates are hints and updates that are sent via JumioScanPartInterface.onUpdate during scanning. You may be required to act on those to improve the capture experience for your users, e.g. FALLBACK or HOLD_STILL.

Link copied to clipboard
FALLBACK

A fallback was triggered on the current JumioScanMode. It can be either initiated by the user JumioFallbackReason.USER_ACTION or automatically due to low performance JumioFallbackReason.LOW_PERFORMANCE or due to a asset loading failed JumioFallbackReason.SCANNING_ERROR. The JumioFallbackReason is delivered in the data parameter of JumioScanPartInterface.onUpdate.
